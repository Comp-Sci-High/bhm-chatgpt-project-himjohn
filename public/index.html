La Concha Motel

The La Concha Motel was a motel that opened in 1961 and closed in 2004. It was designed by architect Paul Williams who was one of the first prominent African American architects in the United States and was also the architect who designed the first LAX theme building by Paul R. Williams...

Fun Facts about Paul R. Williams

  • Fun 1: Paul R. Williams was the first African-American architect to become a member of the American Institute of Architects (AIA).
  • Fact 2: He designed over 2,000 buildings during his career, including many iconic structures in Los Angeles.
  • S 3: Williams became a mentor to many African-American architects, helping to pave the way for future generations in the field.
